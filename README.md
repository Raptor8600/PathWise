# PathWise 📈

**Personal Finance Dumbed Down · Easy Math Explained · No Data Is Saved**

PathWise is a comprehensive suite of personal finance tools designed to centralize and simplify financial planning. Our mission is to demystify complex financial concepts by providing clear calculations accompanied by intuitive, plain-language explanations, empowering you to make informed decisions with confidence.

Explore the live project here: **[https://raptor8600.github.io/PathWise/](https://raptor8600.github.io/PathWise/)**

---

## 🚀 Features

PathWise includes several essential financial calculators to help you plan your future:

- **Compound Growth:** Calculate how your savings grow over time with interest and monthly contributions.
- **Debt Payoff:** See how long it will take to pay off your debt and how much interest you'll pay.
- **Credit Health & Factors:** Understand the key factors that determine your credit score.
- **Loan Payment:** Estimate monthly payments and total costs for car, personal, or home loans.
- **Savings Goal:** Determine exactly how much you need to save each month to reach your target.
- **Inflation:** Visualize how the purchasing power of your money changes over time.
- **Salary Taxes:** Calculate detailed tax withholdings for 2022. Supports multiple income sources across different states simultaneously (IL, NY, CA, and states with no income tax like TX/FL/WA) with aggregate totals and employer-cost "Taxberg" visualization.

## 💻 How to Use Locally

You can run PathWise directly on your own computer without any server setup:

1.  **Download the project files:**
    - `index.html`
    - `style.css`
    - `script.js`
2.  **Keep them in the same folder.**
3.  **Open `index.html`** in any modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge).

No internet connection is required once the files are downloaded (except for the Chart.js library which is loaded via CDN).
